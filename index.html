The best Gluten-Free Sandwich Bread Recipe

Gluten Free

Dairy Free

Originally this recpie came from Kirari west bake shop in Redondo beach. They sold a gluten free flour. They discontinued the flour and told me it was actually Steve's GF Bread Flour from authentic foods. You'll need a stand mixer for this or really persistent mixing effort. Also works best with a 9 x5 glass loaf pan.

The Recipe from Steve's is different from the Kirari one and the Kirari one is what we've made so here it is:

This is dairy free:

Ingredients:

  • Steve's Bread Flour - 430 grams
  • Warm Oat Milk - 1 1/4 cup
  • Olive Oil - 70 grams
  • Sugar - 25 grams
  • Egg - 1 whole egg
  • Egg White - 1/2 cup
  • Instant dry yeast - 2 teaspoons
  • Warm Water - 1/4 cup

Preparing the Dough:

Start with a bowl and add sugar, warm water, and yeast and let the yeast proof for about 5 minutes. Warm the oat milk to about 115 degrees in the microwave. Put the flour in the stand mixer with a normal paddle (not the dough hook or the wire) and start it on very low add the warm oat milk, the eggs and egg whites, the olive oil and finally the proofed yeast mixture. Mix for 10 minutes at medium speed.

Baking the Bread:

Bake in a preheated over at 375 degrees (350 convection) for approximately 60-70 minutes. When the bread is finished baking, remove from the pan and let cook for 1 hour before cutting.

To make it dairy use milk and butter.
